As a young 25 year-old, touring Europe in Gianna's band at the height of her fame was an amazing, eye-opening experience. Some 30-plus years later, to see the same energy and intensity from her was inspiring - and such a great pleasure throughout the whole production of Sei Nel L’Anima. The album has gone on to great success - and received excellent reviews. I don't doubt she will long continue!
