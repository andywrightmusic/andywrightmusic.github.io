We wrote the song together in a really unscripted way, just sitting on a sofa jamming out words and ideas. Afterwards, she got on a mic and I sat at the piano and we just sort of busked it out.

There’s a real flow to the song – it’s one of my favourite ways to work – not always guaranteed to come together, but when it does it can definitely produce something special.
